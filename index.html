How I work

  • I build before I fully understand. Reading documentation only gets me so far. Most of what I know came from breaking things and fixing them.

  • I use AI as a tool, not a crutch. It helps me bridge gaps and move faster. The thinking, the problem definition, the debugging — that's still mine.

  • I care about the real problem. Nigeria has specific constraints — intermittent infrastructure, informal economies, low trust between strangers. I build with those in mind.

  • I ship imperfect things. A working product with rough edges is more useful than a perfect product that doesn't exist yet.
